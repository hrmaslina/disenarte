How it all began...

The story of Life News began over 7 years ago when four free-thinking bloggers launched their first ever website dedicated to various online publications.

Throughout its many years of development, and several transformations, our blog and its publications have been at the very heart of the communities which they serve - informing, educating, entertaining and inspiring them. Today Life News remains a thriving and innovative community media business, which is one of the leading privately-owned blogs.
